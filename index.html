About Us

Together we can save the planet

At Green Zone, we are dedicated to fostering a greener, more sustainable world. Our passion drives us to curate and showcase eco-friendly activities that inspire positive change. Through collaboration and innovation, we aim to raise awareness about environmental issues and empower individuals to take meaningful action.

Projects

Recycling

July 2023-Today

We are dedicated to transforming the recycling landscape by implementing innovative solutions that make recycling more convenient and impactful. We are committed to establishing a network of easily accessible recycling centers and promoting sustainable practices within local communities.

Windmill Installation

Feb 2022-May 2023

We are focused on installing windmills to harness clean, renewable energy. By strategically placing windmills in areas with high wind speeds, we aim to generate electricity in an environmentally friendly manner. Our goal is to reduce carbon emissions and dependency on non-renewable energy sources.
